INTRO

Carl-Scripts.

Hello! I'm Carlito Jr. M. Rehang., a passionate Full Stack Developer and educator based in Digos City, Davao del Sur. I bring together the power of design, development, and digital strategy to build functional and visually compelling web applications.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from the Southern Philippines Agri-Business and Marine and Aquatic School of Technology (SPAMAST), and I am currently pursuing a Master in Information Technology at the University of Southeastern Philippines (USeP).

As a developer, I specialize in crafting end-to-end solutions using modern frontend frameworks like React and backend technologies such as Laravel and Node.js. I am committed to delivering scalable, secure, and impactful systems that support education, governance, and innovation. I believe in continuous learning, purposeful coding, and technology that improves lives.
